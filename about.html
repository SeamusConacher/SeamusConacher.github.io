Biography

Seamus Conacher is an Edinburgh based sound engineer, musician and programmer, with a particular interest on the area where all three disciplines overlap. He graduated with a 2.1 honours degree from the University of Edinburgh in 2015, focusing on acousmatic composition and audio programming.

He has recorded orchestras, a Doomjazz band, and Rubix cubes, as well as performing with multiple bands. From 2011 until 2014, he was the guitarist in T.J. Muller and the Dixie/The Gramophone Jass Band, before leaving in order to complete his degree. During that time, he toured the UK and europe, recorded an album, appeared on national radio and television, and performed at both the Edinburgh Jazz and Blues and Edinburgh Fringe Festival
