Bio

I am an Edinburgh based sound engineer, musician and programmer, with a particular interest in the area where all three disciplines overlap. My skills include:

  • Programming in:
    • C/C++
    • Ruby (including Rails)
    • Python
    • JavaScript (including jQuery)
    • Matlab
    • Common Lisp
    • PureData and MaxMSP
    • HTML/CSS
  • Sound recording/Band recording
  • Sound mixing
  • Rock and jazz guitar

I graduated with a 2.1 honours degree in Music Technology from the University of Edinburgh in 2015, focusing on acousmatic composition and audio programming.
